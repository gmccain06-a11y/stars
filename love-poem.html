In Your Eyes

In your eyes, I see the stars that light up my darkest nights,

In your smile, I find the warmth that melts away my frights.

In your voice, I hear the melody that my heart longs to keep,

In your touch, I feel the comfort that helps me fall asleep.

You are the sun that rises in my morning sky,

You are the moon that guides me when the day is nigh.

You are the anchor that holds me in life's stormy sea,

You are the treasure that means more than all to me.

So take this heart, it beats for you alone,

Take this love, it's yours to keep and own.

Forever and always, yours
