Missing You

Every night I look up at the stars and wonder if you're looking at them too. The distance between us feels like an ocean, but my heart finds a way to cross it, just to be with you in my dreams.

Every moment without you feels like an eternity, yet I hold onto the memories we've created. They're my anchor in this sea of missing you.

Until we meet again, know that you're always in my thoughts, in my heart, and in every wish I make upon a falling star.

Forever yours
